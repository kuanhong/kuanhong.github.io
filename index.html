About me

About Me

UX Designer who brings a technical perspective.

Hi there from sunny Singapore! I am Rollin, a passionate UX designer with a background in software engineering. My journey from being a systems analyst and software developer in the tech world has provided me with a unique perspective, allowing me to bridge the gap between user experience design and technical implementation. I am deeply committed to crafting delightful and intuitive user experiences that not only bring smiles to users' faces but also ensure seamless functionality and efficiency.

By leveraging data-driven insights and a deep understanding of engineering principles, I strive to create designs that are not only visually appealing but also technically sound. My goal is to blend creativity with analytical thinking, producing designs that engage users while solving real-world problems. With a keen eye for detail and a holistic approach to design, I am excited to collaborate on projects that make user interactions as seamless and enjoyable as possible. Let's work together to bring these visions to life!
